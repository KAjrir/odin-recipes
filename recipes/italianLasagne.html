Italian Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish, layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and cheese. It's a hearty, satisfying meal perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets: 12 pieces, cooked
  • Ground beef: 500g
  • Onion: 1, finely chopped
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, minced
  • Tomato sauce: 2 cups
  • Spices: 1 tsp oregano, 1/2 tsp basil, salt, and pepper to taste
  • Béchamel sauce: 2 cups (made with butter, flour, and milk)
  • Mozzarella cheese: 200g, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese: 1/2 cup, grated
  • Olive oil: 2 tbsp

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce: Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onions and garlic, then add ground beef. Cook until browned, then mix in tomato sauce and spices.
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets: Boil lasagna sheets until al dente and drain.
  3. Layer the lasagna: In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, then lasagna sheets, béchamel, and cheese. Repeat until all ingredients are used, finishing with cheese on top.
  4. Bake: Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Bake for 30-40 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly.
  5. Serve: Let it cool for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
