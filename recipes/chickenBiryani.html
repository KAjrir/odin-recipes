Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic South Asian dish made with spiced rice, marinated chicken, and fragrant spices. It is slow-cooked to perfection, layering rice and chicken for a delicious blend of tastes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 500g, marinated
  • Rice: 2 cups basmati, soaked
  • Onions: 2, sliced
  • Tomatoes: 2, chopped
  • Yogurt: 1/2 cup
  • Spices: 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp coriander, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chili powder, 3-4 cloves, and 2 cardamom pods
  • Ginger and garlic paste: 1 tbsp
  • Fresh cilantro and mint: Handful, chopped
  • Ghee or oil: 3 tbsp
  • Saffron: Soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk
  • Water or chicken stock: 2 cups

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices. Let it marinate for 1 hour.
  2. Cook the chicken: Heat ghee/oil in a pot, saut√© onions until golden, then add tomatoes and the marinated chicken. Cook until tender.
  3. Parboil the rice: Cook the soaked rice in salted boiling water for 5 minutes, then drain.
  4. Layer the biryani: In a pot, layer the cooked chicken and parboiled rice. Sprinkle saffron milk, fresh cilantro, and mint between layers.
  5. Steam cook: Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes until the rice is fully cooked.
  6. Serve: Gently mix and serve with yogurt or raita.
